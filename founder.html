Mr. Kassoum
Coulibaly

He is an economist, strategist, intellectual, technology, economic, and social entrepreneur driven and committed to global economic progress, sustainable development, and universal peace promotion with over 21 years of experience working with Youth and Women across Africa and the World. He is dedicating his life to promoting and protecting Human Rights, Women's Rights, Gender Equality, and the Empowerment of Women Worldwide by engaging men and boys.

Our Founder

  • Mr. Coulibaly is the Founder, President, and CEO of the Afrika Global Gender Academy (AGGA), a branch of the Afrika Global Group (AG Group) LLC USA, a member of the MenEngage Global Alliance, and also the National Coordinator of MenEngage Network Mali and Vice-Chair of the Steering Committee of MenEngage Africa Alliance. He is also a UN Women's HeForShe Initiative Member and the Co-Founder of the African Development Group (ADG), the African Mining Alliance (AMA), the Alliance for African Renaissance (ARA), and the African Youth Center (AYC).
  • He has worked for several national and international NGOs and United Nations (UN) organizations like UNESCO, UNDP, UNV, and UNCTAD in Africa. He has also engaged with some famous global organizations like the International Association of Students in Economics and Commerce (AIESEC) and Junior Chamber International (JCI) where he held leadership positions in the past. As an Economic Freedom Fighter (EFF), Mr. Coulibaly is currently supporting the promotion of entrepreneurship among Youth and Women organizations for more than ten (10) years in the issues of information and communication, education and training, technical assistance and consulting, studies, and research.
  • In 2011-2012, he served as the Economic and Commercial Counselor for Africa at the Sichuan Supplier Chamber of Commerce (SSCC) in China and continued to develop economic, social, and cultural relations between Africa and China. In 2015-2016, he was selected as one of the UN Women's Empower Women Program Global Champions for Women's Economic Empowerment (WEE) where he demonstrated his ability to support rural women entrepreneurs. In 2016-2017, he was one of the UN Women's Empower Women Program Mentors in the Business Development Group (BDG). In 2018, he joined the Side By Side Faith Movement for Gender Justice as a key member of its National Steering Group in Mali, the Sub-Regional Steering Group in West Africa, and the Global Steering Group in the United Kingdom (UK). In 2019, he was appointed as one of the members of the Consultative Council of UN Women in Mali.
  • He obtained an Associates Degree in Economics and Development at the University of Bouaké (Côte d'Ivoire), Bachelor's and Master's Degrees in Management at the University of Bamako (Mali) in 2000. He also obtained a Postgraduate Diploma in Human Rights from the Centre Universitaire Henry Dunant (CUHD) in Geneva (Switzerland) in 2008, a Certificate in Mineral Policy and Contract Negotiation from the University of Johannesburg (UJ) in South Africa in 2013, a Certificate in Business and Human Rights in 2016, and a Certificate in Advanced Course in Human Rights on the role of Men and Boys in Achieving Gender Equality in 2018 at the University of Pretoria (UP) in South Africa. In 2020, he obtained the prestigious GenderPro Certificate at the Global Women's Institute (GWI) of The George Washington University (GWU).
  • Mr. Kassoum Coulibaly speaks both French and English.
